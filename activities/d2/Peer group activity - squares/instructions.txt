1. OBSERVE: Take a look at the composition in this folder.

2. IDENTIFY: List the key information points that visitors are likely seeking (time? place?). Assign values (eg. 1-10) according to their importance to the average visitor. [ 5 min ]

3. ANALYZE: Now, look at the actual design. Go through each of the principles of design and describe how they are used in the composition. Assign values (1-10) according to the actual visual importance of the elements as you see it in the design.  [ 10 min ]

4. EVALUATE: Does the expected importance match up with the actual designed importance? Are they using the principles of design in a way that emphasizes the relevant information? In what way would you change the design? How would you change it so that the artist names have equal importance? [ 10 min ]
